## Key Differentiators

### Free
- 3 active personal tapes, 1 active shared tape (view only)
- Can receive and contribute to collaborative tapes (no creation)
- Exports carry watermark
- No music library or AI music
- Can save tapes to device

### Plus
- Unlimited personal tapes
- 20 shared + 20 collaborative tapes per month
- Full collaboration controls (invite, promote co-admin, admin controls)
- Watermark-free exports
- 12k track music library
- .tape file generation
- View only tape expiry control

### Together
- Everything in Plus
- 50 shared + 50 collaborative tapes per month (up from 20)
- AI mood-based music
- AI prompt-based music

## Notes

- AI music features (mood + prompt) are the key differentiator pushing users from Plus to Together
- All tiers can receive and view shared/collaborative tapes — only creation/admin is gated
- Sharing limits are monthly, not cumulative, avoiding runaway storage costs
